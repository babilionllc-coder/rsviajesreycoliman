🤔 Travel Agency or Online Booking — Which Wins?

This is the most-asked question at RS Viajes Rey Colimán. The honest answer depends on the trip:

When a Colima travel agency wins

  • ✅ Complex international trips (Europe, Asia, multi-city itineraries)
  • ✅ Groups of 4 or more travelers (we secure group discounts)
  • ✅ Honeymoons or anniversary trips (special-detail coordination)
  • ✅ Graduation trips (full group logistics)
  • ✅ First-time international travelers (visa and document support)

When booking online may be enough

  • ⚠️ Very simple domestic trips (flight + 3-night hotel)
  • ⚠️ You have deep prior experience with that exact destination
  • ⚠️ You have unlimited time to compare 5-6 platforms yourself
