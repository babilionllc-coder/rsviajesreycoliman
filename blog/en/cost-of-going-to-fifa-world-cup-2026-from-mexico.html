🛂 US Visa — The Single Biggest Obstacle

⚠️ Critical: Every match in the USA (most knockout games) requires a valid US tourist visa for Mexican citizens. Without it, you cannot enter the USA — even with World Cup tickets. As of April 2026, US Embassy wait times in Mexico are 12-18 months. If you do not already have a visa or active appointment, your only realistic option is matches in Mexico or Canada.

Matches in Mexico (CDMX, Guadalajara, Monterrey) and Canada (Toronto, Vancouver) require only a valid passport for Mexican citizens. Canada accepts the eTA (electronic, $7 CAD, takes 5 minutes online).
