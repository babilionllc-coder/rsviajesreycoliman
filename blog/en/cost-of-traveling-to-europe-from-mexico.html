✈️ Flights from Mexico to Europe

The flight is usually the largest single line item. Round-trip prices vary significantly by season and airline:

  • Low season (Jan-Mar, Nov): $550-$850 USD
  • Shoulder season (Apr-May, Sep-Oct): $750-$1,100 USD
  • High season (Jun-Aug, Dec): $1,000-$1,500 USD

Best airlines from Mexico City to Europe (2026): Aeroméxico (direct to Madrid + Paris), Iberia, Air France, KLM, Lufthansa, British Airways. From Guadalajara/Monterrey you typically connect through MEX, Madrid, Houston or Atlanta.

🍽️ Daily Expenses — Food, Transport, Tours

Meals per day (mid-range): $30-$70 USD per person. Breakfast at hotel often included; lunch $12-$20 USD; dinner at a regular restaurant $25-$40 USD. Tip culture varies — usually 5-10% in Western Europe.

Public transport: $8-$15 USD/day for unlimited metro+bus. Inter-city trains: Paris-London $90 USD; Rome-Florence $40 USD; Madrid-Barcelona $50 USD.

Tours and admissions: Vatican Museums $35 USD, Eiffel Tower $30 USD, Sagrada Familia $30 USD, Acropolis $25 USD. A guided full-day tour is typically $80-$140 USD per person.

📅 When to Travel: The Cheapest Months

  • Cheapest: January, February, November — flights up to 40% cheaper than August.
  • Best weather/value balance: April-May or September-October.
  • Most expensive: mid-June through August (school break) and Christmas/New Year week.
  • Book 4-6 months in advance for the best fare. Last-minute rates from Mexico to Europe are rarely below $1,200 USD.
